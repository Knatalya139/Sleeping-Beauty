Sleeping Beauty Summary:

Sleeping Beauty is a fairytale story by Charles Perrault. It is about a young princess who as a child is cursed by a evil witch named Maleficent to die on her 16th birthday by pricking her finger on a spinning wheel. Three good fairys try to reverse the curse by saying she won't die but just fall asleep and she can be woken by a true loves kiss by a handsome prince. The princess's parents, still frightened for their child ask the three good fairys to take care of her and hide her in the woods until her 16th birthday. The three fairys decide to name the baby princess Aurora and raise as if she was just a regular peasent. Aurora grows up in the woods and one day she meets a handsome prince in the woods and falls in love with him. Soon it is her 16th birthday and her fairy godmothers have to explain that she is a princess and that she will return to the castle again. Aurora goes to the castle and stubles upon a room with a mysterious green light. The light leads to a spinning wheel and she pokes her finger on it and falls in to an eternal sleep. When the fairys find out they put everyone else in the kingdom asleep too so that when Aurora wakes up she won't feel like she missed out. The handsome prince from the woods hears about this and tries to find Aurora but while he does he runs in to Maleficent. The evil witch turns in to a dragon and attacks the prince but with a little help from the three good fairys he slays the dragon and kills Maleficent. Once he reaches Aurora's castle he gives her a true love's kiss and she wakes. So does everyone else! The prince and Aurora get married and live happily ever after.
