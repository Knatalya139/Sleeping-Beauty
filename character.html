Sleeping Beauty Characters:

Aurora

Also known as Briar Rose, Aurora is graceful and kind. She knows that a wonderful future awaits, if you just have the courage to dream it. Aurora enjoys using her imagination and sharing stories with her forest friends. She is also loyal in her relationships -- to her animal friends, her fairies, and her kingdom.

The Three Fairy Godmothers

Flora, Fauna, and Merryweather are Princess Aurora's fairy godmothers and adoptive aunts in Sleeping Beauty. Flora is the girly one, Merryweather is the tomboyish one and Fauna is in between. Flora and Merryweather constantly argue over the color of Aurora's dress.

Prince Philip

Prince Philip is strong, brave and charming. He is also kind to the animals and the birds just like Aurora. He is the Prince of another kingdom, not the one Aurora is from. The reason his dad and Aurora's dad want them to get married is because then the two kingdoms can make peace.

King Stefan and Queen Leah

King Stefan and Queen Leah are Aurora's parents. They sent her to live with the fairy godmothers so that she would be safe and stay away from the witch Maleficent. Even though they sent her away they still missed Aurora all the time and hoped she would come back.

Maleficent

Maleficent is the villain in Sleeping Beauty. She is the witch who cursed Aurora to prick her hand on a spinning wheel on her 16th birthday and have a eternal sleep. In the end Prince Philip kills her and good conquers evil like in most fairytales.
